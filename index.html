About Group

The Aeron Group is a leading, Punjab based, financial group, with over 8 years of experience in developing residential and commercial projects and Financial solution for all your needs either your Home loan, Vehicle loan, Corporate loan, Car loan etc. Established in 2010, it has a diverse portfolio of over 150 completed projects. The Aeron Group started with partnership in premium locations of Punjab like NK Sharma, Sushma Builder, CCC, GBP, Omex, TDI, ERO City. The Group has extensive experience in development of custom-built homes, with focus on timelines, quality and construction management.
